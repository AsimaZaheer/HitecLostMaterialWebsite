Help & FAQ

Account & Login

How do I create an account?
Click on "Sign Up" and fill in your details to create an account.
I forgot my password, how to reset it?
Click on “Forgot Password” on the login page and follow the steps.
Can I update my profile information?
Yes, go to your profile settings and edit the information as needed.

Using the Platform / Services

How do I post a report?
Navigate to “Report an Issue” page and fill out the required fields.
How to search lost/found items?
Use the search bar in the dashboard to find reported items.
Can I edit or delete my submission?
Yes, you can manage your reports from your account dashboard.

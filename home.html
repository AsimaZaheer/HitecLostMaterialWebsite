About

This portal is important as it provides a centralized and efficient platform to manage lost
and found items at HITEC, ensuring transparency, accuracy, and faster recovery compared
to the manual WhatsApp process.”

How Work

  1. 1.Students or staff submit details of the lost or found item through the portal.
  2. 2.Supervisors check and approve the report to ensure authenticity.
  3. 3.Users can browse or search the database to find matching lost/found items.
  4. 4.Once verified, the rightful owner can claim the item and recover it easily.
